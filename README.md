# Clearforge

**Human-led. AI-empowered.**

Turning human input into clear, usable systems.

## Current project

Clearforge Daily AI Brief Builder.

## Core promise

AI news is noisy. Clearforge turns it into clear, usable daily learning and content.

## End goal

Build a controlled daily publishing workflow:

1. Collect reputable AI news sources.
2. Select useful stories for practical readers.
3. Generate one human-review article draft.
4. Verify facts, links, claims, and wording.
5. Repurpose the approved article into social formats.
6. Publish or schedule posts to selected channels only after human approval.

Target channels:

- Blog article
- Facebook post
- Pinterest pin
- YouTube Short

## Current phase

Foundation only.

This repo currently contains:

- brand notes
- prompt foundation
- article template
- social repurpose template
- editorial rules
- workflow plan
- manual 7-day test plan

Full automation is intentionally not active yet.

## Publishing rule

Clearforge must never auto-publish raw AI output. Every article and social post must be reviewed by a human before publication.

## Brand separation

Clearforge is a separate brand. Public-facing Clearforge material must not mention or link to any unrelated publishing imprint, product line, or previous business identity.
