# Clearforge Brand Notes

## Name

Clearforge

## Tagline

Human-led. AI-empowered.

## Line

Turning human input into clear, usable systems.

## Core promise

AI news is noisy. Clearforge turns it into clear, usable daily learning and content.

## What Clearforge is for

Clearforge helps practical learners, creators, freelancers, and small businesses turn AI news and AI-assisted workflows into clear systems they can understand, test, and use.

Primary lanes:

- AI learning
- AI news explained plainly
- AI-assisted workflows
- clear systems
- articles
- content repurposing
- automation

## Voice

Clearforge should sound:

- human-led
- plain English
- practical
- useful, not hype-led
- educational
- careful with claims
- grounded in what normal people can actually do next

## Avoid

- unsupported claims
- AI hype
- fear-based framing
- "AI will replace everyone" content
- medical, legal, or financial advice
- pretending drafts are final facts
- copying article wording
- auto-publishing without human review

## Brand separation

Public-facing Clearforge material must not mention unrelated brands, product names, repositories, shops, or imprints.

If a technical repo owner, deployment account, or legacy tool has an unrelated name, do not expose it in public-facing copy, screenshots, documentation, or marketing.
