## Story comparison

### The White House is building a cyber coordination layer for AI-era vulnerabilities

- Audience: Small business operators, IT/security leads, and teams using AI in customer-facing or operational workflows
- Interest signal: The White House said it created GOLD EAGLE, a coordination clearinghouse for vulnerability detection and patch prioritization across critical infrastructure
- Stop reason: This is not a product demo; it is a real-world signal that AI-era systems need clear handoffs and response ownership
- Payoff: A simple rule for production workflows: add alert routing, human approval and rollback planning before scaling automation
- Proof: White House-confirmed GOLD EAGLE initiative with Treasury, DHS/CISA, the Department of War and industry partners
- Overall score: 8/10
- Reason: Strong practical relevance for operators and small teams, with a clear takeaway about alerts, approvals and rollback. Slightly less searchable than a consumer-facing AI topic, but very usable and discussion-friendly.
