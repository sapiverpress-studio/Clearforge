## TikTok Script

Can your AI finish a task with fewer edits?
OpenAI says AI should be measured by useful work completed, cost per successful task, dependability, and whether it scales well. That is a better test than chasing flashy outputs. If you use AI for work, pick one weekly task and judge it by the finish line: time saved, retries avoided, and human cleanup reduced. The stop reason is simple — a cheap draft is not a good result if it takes three rounds to finish. What task would you test first: customer replies or research notes?
